FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about your C Serpents rack or incubator.

Rack Setup
Do I need a thermostat?+

Yes — always. Never plug a rack directly into an outlet. The flexwatt heat tape does get hot, and without a thermostat you risk overheating your animals. A thermostat is non‑negotiable.

How do I verify tub temperatures?+

Use a digital temp gun. Check the heated area of each tub before putting any animals in. The thermostat is a guide — the temp gun gives you the actual reading.

Where do I place the thermostat probe?+

For hatchling and shoebox racks, place the probe on the aluminum cover or heat tape. For all other racks, place it directly on the heat tape of the middle shelf. You can run the wire through the front of the rack or cut a small opening in the back panel.

Can I run multiple racks on one thermostat?+

Yes — but only the same type of rack, up to the thermostat's wattage capacity. For example, up to 6 Sub‑Adult V‑28 racks on a VE‑200 (700W). You cannot mix rack types on one thermostat unless using a dual‑probe unit like the VE‑300 2X.

Do I need to drill holes in the tubs?+

Our racks have enough gap for adequate airflow. Holes are only needed if you have a persistent condensation problem. Every environment is different.

Can I stack my racks?+

Yes. Stack the same type of rack only. Dowel pins are included — use 2 in opposite corners. Do not mix different rack types when stacking.

Orders & Shipping
How long does shipping take?+

Approximately 3 weeks from the date of order. This may vary slightly based on show schedule, order volume, or supply chain.

My rack has minor scratches. What do I do?+

Minor surface scratches can be covered with a small amount of black shoe polish. They don't affect performance.

The back screws weren't installed. Is something missing?+

Back screws are removed for shipping. Re‑install with a standard Phillips screwdriver — hand tighten only.

Thermostats
On/off vs. proportional — what's the difference?+

On/off cuts power at the set temp and restores when it drops. Proportional sends small pulses for steadier temps. For racks, on/off is fine. For incubators, use proportional for better hatch rates.

Which thermostat do you recommend?+

For most racks: VE‑200 or VE‑300. For two different rack types or rack + incubator simultaneously: VE‑300 2X (dual probe). For the Combo Rack: VE‑300 2X is required.
