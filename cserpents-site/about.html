C Serpents started where most great things start — with a passion for reptiles and a frustration with products that didn't meet the standard.

Over 20 years ago, Chris Nettles began building rack systems and incubators for his own collection. Word spread. Keepers wanted what he was building. C Serpents was born.

Today, we manufacture and ship rack systems, Hot Box incubators, and accessories to keepers and breeders across the country. We've attended hundreds of reptile shows and earned a reputation for quality that speaks for itself.

We're not a corporation. We're keepers who happen to make the best rack systems in the business. When you order from C Serpents, you're dealing directly with the people who built your product — and who stand behind it.
